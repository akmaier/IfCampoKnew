## **Termination regulations for the Master’s Degree Program for Professional Development in Human Rights at the Faculty of Humanities, Social Sciences, and Theology of Friedrich-Alexander-Universität Erlangen-Nürnberg (FAU) dated January 15, 2026** 

Based on the currently valid versions of Section 9 (1) in conjunction with Section 80 (1) and Section 84 (2) Bavarian Higher Education Innovation Act, FAU enacts the following regulations: 

## **Section 1 Termination of the Degree Program** 

1The Master’s degree program for professional development in Human Rights at the Faculty of Humanities, Social Sciences, and Theology at FAU shall be terminated as of winter semester 2026/2027[2] At the latest at the beginning of winter semester 2026/2027 (October 1, 2026), students will no longer be able to enroll or be admitted to study this degree program in either the first or any later semester.[3] This shall not affect the re-registration of students pursuant to Section 2 (1) who are completing their active studies at FAU. 

## **Section 2 Transitory Provision** 

1Students who are enrolled in the Master’s degree program for professional development in Human Rights at the Faculty of Humanities, Social Sciences, and Theology at FAU in winter semester 2026/2027 shall have the opportunity to complete their degree program as planned.[2] Examinations in the degree program stated in sentence 1 shall be offered for the last time in summer semester 2028. 

## **Section 3 Termination of the Degree Program and Examination Regulations** 

1The degree program and examination regulations for the Master’s degree program for professional development in Human Rights at the Faculty of Humanities, Social Sciences, and Theology at FAU – **FPOHR** – dated September 28, 2023, last amended by statute of December 19, 2024, shall be amended as follows: 

In Section 29, the following new paragraph 5 is added after paragraph 4: 

“[1] These degree program and examination regulations shall become invalid as of September 30, 2028.[2] Examinations pursuant to these degree program and examination regulations shall be offered for the last time in summer semester 2028.” 

## **Section 4 Legal Validity** 

These regulations shall come into effect on February 1, 2026.
